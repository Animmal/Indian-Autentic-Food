Paneer Popcorn

A perfect starter for parties and special days, here is a detailed recipe for Paneer Popcorn with images and a video. If you have all the ingredients on hand, Paneer Popcorn is as easy to make as a cup of Tea. And it is so tasty that you couldn't just stop eating.

  • PREPARATION

STEPS

Mix all the ingredients for coating and make a batter of thin consistency. Add Paneer pieces and mix until all the pieces get well coated. Take each piece of Paneer and roll it in Bread Crumbs a couple of times and ensure it is coated well. After all the pieces have been coated, keep them in the fridge for 30 minutes. After 30 minutes, put the Paneer pieces in hot oil, on a reduced flame and fry until they turn golden and crispy. Remove and serve.
